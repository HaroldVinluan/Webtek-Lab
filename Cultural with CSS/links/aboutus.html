About Us

Act with great leadership, quality service, and wisdom.

Preamble

We the students of Saint Louis University, adhering to Christian Principles, imploring the aid of Almighty God, recognizing the rights and responsibilities of students, in order to establish a nationalistic, democratic, autonomous and representative student government that shall uphold the fundamental rights of the students, and other sectors of the society, in order to foster and enhance the individual and social personality of the Louisian. Do ordain and promulgate this constitution.

Mission

The KASAMA/SSC recognizes its role in advancing the welfare, ideals, and aspirations of the studentry, and the role of the SLU Administration, Faculty, and Personnel in all aspects of human development.

Vission

The KASAMA/SSC is the autonomous, democratic, nationalist, and representative student government of Saint Louis University, The authority of which emanates from the student

Objectives

  • Be the vanguard of fundamental rights, interest and general welfare of the students.
  • Promote cordial relation among the students and with the administration, faculty and employees of Saint Louis University
  • Act as the sole legitimate voice who shall represent the position of the student to the SLU Admin, other education institutions, and to government agencies in all matters affecting their rights and interest comforming with the official policies, rules, and objectives of the Saint Louis University.
